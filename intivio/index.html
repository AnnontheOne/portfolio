— First Look · intivio.com

Three moves, day one.

I spent time with the site before this call. Here's where I'd start.

Move 01
Off Squarespace.
The site lives inside template constraints. Migrating to Next.js + headless CMS means we own the codebase — 3D, motion, custom interactions, performance budgets all become ours to control instead of fights with someone else's renderer.
Move 02
Prometheus as a product.
Right now Prometheus AI reads like a marketing page. The opportunity is making it a surface you can actually touch — embedded demos, a live chatbot on the site, dashboard previews. The site becomes proof, not promise.
Move 03
One language.
Consumer and Business run as two parallel hierarchies. A unified design system with smart context switching cuts navigation in half, makes the brand feel bigger and more deliberate, and gives the team one place to ship updates.
